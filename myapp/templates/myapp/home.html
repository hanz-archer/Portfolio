Experience

Senior High School Work Immersion Program

FJR IT Solutions | January 2020 - April 2020

As part of the Senior High School curriculum, the Work Immersion Program provides students with real-world experience and hands-on training in their chosen fields. I was assigned to FJR IT Solutions, where I gained practical knowledge in the IT and technical services sector.

My responsibilities included working as a shop attendant, assisting customers with basic technical needs and troubleshooting. I also installed point-to-point (P2P) WiFi connections, ensuring network setups were correctly implemented for home and small business use.

Additionally, I provided PC services, such as hardware installation, software setup, and maintenance. This hands-on experience greatly enhanced my technical skills and deepened my understanding of IT solutions.

2024 POPCEN-CBMS Field Enumerator

Philippine Statistics Authority | June 2024 - September 2024

As part of the 2024 Population Census and Community-Based Monitoring System (POPCEN-CBMS) conducted by the Philippine Statistics Authority, I was tasked with collecting demographic, social, and economic data from households. This data is critical for national planning, development programs, and the formulation of policies aimed at improving the quality of life for Filipinos.

I was assigned to Barangay Talaytay Enumeration Areas 1000 and 4000, where I conducted door-to-door interviews, gathered household data, and ensured the accuracy of the information collected. My role involved identifying and addressing discrepancies in the data and assisting respondents in understanding the importance of their participation in the census.
